About Me

I am a locally grown Winnipeg graphic designer and artist. I graduated from Red River College's Graphic Design program in 2018, and spent the next several years working in the printing, marketing, and signage industries. I've worked directly with local companies like BDI, Thom Brokerage, and Wolseley Strata, as well as numerous other local businesses—including larger organizations like Cancer Care, IntouchCX, and the Royal Canadian Mint. More recently, I've been working as a muralist, and my work with The TOBA Centre Bison Statues can still be viewed around the city. If you're interested in getting anything designed—be it on paper, bags, shirts, signage, vinyl stickers, backdrops, pamphlets, or anything else—just let me know and I'd be happy to give you a hand!
