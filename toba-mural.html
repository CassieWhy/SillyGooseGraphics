Nuit Blanch by The Cube

Excitement Returns, Nuit Blanch by The Cube

This design was inspired by my experience at The Cube during Nuit Blanch 2024. It was the first time since the pandemic that I truly felt Winnipeg coming back to life. The vibrant colours and the incredible atmosphere of the crowd were unreal, and it made me feel incredibly proud to be a Winnipegger.
